EFT Extension Configuration

Configure your Escape from Tarkov player AID to display your loadout data.

How to find your AID:

  1. Go to https://tarkov.dev/players
  2. Search for your in-game name (IGN)
  3. Click on your player profile
  4. Copy the numbers from the URL (e.g., if the URL is https://tarkov.dev/players/regular/2826068, your AID is 2826068)
